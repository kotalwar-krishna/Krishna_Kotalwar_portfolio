India's Love on Street Food

India has always loved its street food and any city you visit in the country will be lined with vendors selling local favorite street food. On the streets, you can find meals, nashta, fraky and refreshing drinks like chai, coffee or fresh-fruits juice.

Indian street food is often spicy, somewhere sweet, sometimes oily and always delicious when you eat always you love it. Indian street food have one more quality It's also same cost for all classes of citizens.
